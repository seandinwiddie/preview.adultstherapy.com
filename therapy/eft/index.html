Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT) Therapy

Energy Psychology for Trauma, Pain, and Emotional Healing

What is Emotional Freedom Technique?

Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT), also known as "tapping," is an evidence-based energy psychology method that combines cognitive therapy with acupressure. This powerful technique involves tapping on specific meridian points while focusing on emotional issues, helping to restore the body's energy balance and promote rapid healing.

As a certified EFT professional, I help individuals process trauma, reduce emotional and physical pain, and address cravings and addictions through this gentle yet effective approach.

How EFT Works

The Science Behind EFT

EFT is based on the understanding that emotional distress is caused by disruptions in the body's energy system. By tapping on specific acupressure points while focusing on the issue at hand, we can:

  • Calm the amygdala (fear center of the brain)
  • Reduce cortisol levels and stress responses
  • Restore proper energy flow through meridian pathways
  • Create new neural pathways for healing
  • Process traumatic memories without re-traumatization

What to Expect in EFT Sessions

During an EFT session, we will:

  1. Identify the issue: Clearly define what you want to work on
  2. Rate the intensity: Measure your distress level on a 0-10 scale
  3. Setup statement: Acknowledge the problem while affirming self-acceptance
  4. Tapping sequence: Tap on specific meridian points while focusing on the issue
  5. Check progress: Re-evaluate the intensity and continue as needed
  6. Positive installation: Reinforce positive beliefs and feelings
