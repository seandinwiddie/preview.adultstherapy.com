Learn more about me

My name is Elaine Dinwiddie.

I have the training to help veterans and military personnel.

I am an Adult and Couples therapist specializing in helping people who would love to be set free from psychological stress, relationship issues, and the pressures of life.

Why therapy? 

Because I spend my working life helping people achieve meaningful, significant change, and positive core healing.

So I practice one-on-one and group, telehealth counseling in order to feature the successful progress of people like you toward personal growth in each of their unique journeys.

My Story

Arriving from within a framework that is based on the understanding of how trauma affects people’s lives, I can help with depression, anxiety, bipolar, stress management, ADHD, PTSD, phobias, Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs), trauma, and problematic relationships.

Creating a safe space for healing and growth through evidence-based therapeutic approaches

Having a background in the Trauma-Informed Care (TIC) framework, I work with people who want to live happier and healthier lives.

The TIC is an approach to be applied where an individual has a history of trauma.

I have received a bachelor’s degree in Psychology, a master’s in Human Services: Marriage and Family, and a master’s in Professional Counseling; from Liberty University, in Lynchburg, VA.

I am an EMDR II Practitioner, Gottman Method Couples Therapy Level II, and a Certified Clinical Trauma Professional (CCTP).

Professional Credentials & Specializations

Gottman Method Training & Certification

As a Level 2 Gottman Method Couples Therapist, I have completed advanced training in this evidence-based approach to couples therapy. This certification has equipped me with specialized skills to help couples build stronger relationships through:

  • Assessment techniques to identify relationship strengths and areas for growth
  • Methods to help couples manage conflict more effectively
  • Strategies to build friendship, intimacy, and shared meaning in relationships
  • Tools to help couples overcome the "Four Horsemen" of criticism, contempt, defensiveness, and stonewalling

EMDR & Trauma Qualifications

My EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) Level II training provides me with advanced therapeutic tools to help clients process traumatic memories and experiences. As a Certified Clinical Trauma Professional (CCTP), I offer:

  • Specialized trauma-informed care using the latest research-based approaches
  • EMDR therapy for processing traumatic memories and reducing their emotional impact
  • Skills to identify and address trauma symptoms that may manifest as anxiety, depression, or relationship difficulties
  • Expertise in treating both single-incident trauma and complex developmental trauma

Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT) Certification

As a certified Emotional Freedom Technique professional, I utilize this powerful energy psychology approach to help clients achieve rapid relief from emotional and physical distress. EFT combines elements of cognitive therapy with acupressure techniques to help:

  • Process and release traumatic memories and emotional blockages
  • Reduce emotional and physical pain symptoms
  • Address cravings and addictive behaviors
  • Alleviate anxiety, phobias, and PTSD symptoms
  • Improve emotional regulation and stress management

Experience & Areas of Specialization
